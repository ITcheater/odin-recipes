Apple Roll Donuts

Description: Try these apple cider stuffed Hawaiian rolls that are reminiscent of apple donuts.

Ingredients

  1. 1 (12-count) package Hawaiian rolls
  2. 1 cup apple pie filling
  3. 2 (0.74 ounce) pouches apple cider powdered drink mix (such as Alpine Spiced Apple Cider)
  4. 5 tablespoons butter, divided
  5. 1/2 cup sugar
  6. 1 teaspoon cinnamon
